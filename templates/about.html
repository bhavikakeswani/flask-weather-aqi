About SkySQI

SkySQI is a modern web application that provides real-time weather and Air Quality Index (AQI) information for cities across the world. Our mission is to make environmental data accessible, interactive, and useful for everyone.

Using trusted data sources such as OpenWeatherMap and AirVisual API, we bring you up-to-date insights on temperature, humidity, wind, UV index, and pollution levels.

Our Mission

To help individuals, families, and communities make better decisions for their health and environment by providing accurate weather and air quality insights.

Our Team

SkySQI is developed by a group of passionate developers and data enthusiasts who believe that technology can play a key role in environmental awareness.
